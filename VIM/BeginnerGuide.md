### VIM Key Concept

* Operators

* Text Objects
* Motions

```txt
[count][operator][text object / motion]
```

## Navigation

## MacOS Related

> To copy and paste from your PRIMARY (on OS X, your clipboard) you use the * register

* Copy to clipboard: `"*yy`
* Paste from clipboard: `"*p`
